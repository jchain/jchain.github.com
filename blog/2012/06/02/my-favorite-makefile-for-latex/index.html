My Favorite Makefile for LaTeX

Jun 2nd, 2012

Finally, my search for a good Makefile for LaTeX  document writing  was ended by this solution. You may have heard about latex-makefile or latex-mk or other LaTeX makefiles. But either they are too complicated, dependent on other external programs, need to install before use or not  powerful enough to get the most jobs done. Here, this latex-makefile is light-weight, self-contained, zero-dependent and powerful to deal with most cases. Simply download it, drop into the same directory with your LaTeX source file and you are ready to go. Its colorful output really makes error-checking much easier. I like it very much. Highly recommend it.
